  • About Me

       Hello! My name is Milan Hronský
       I am an electrical engineer researching the propagation
       of stochastic signals through linear systems. I Work
       towards better integration of renewable energy in the grid.

A common problem of renewable energy is its intermittency. The share of fossile and nuclear energy in the grid will remain high, unless the renewable power is stable over time. The first stage of its stabilizing is smoothing the renewable power on a low aggregation level.
I have developed a lossless filter of intermittent power, accumulating a minimum of energy. It can provide synthetic inertia to the grid on days with low solar intermittency, as a by-product.
The novel smoothing method SPLPF is highly effective with low [Wh] capacity energy storage systems (ESS) providing high relative power [W/Wh] like e.g. flywheel (FESS) or supercapacitor (EDLC). I have a pending patent app. No. PCT/SK2022/050014 in the smoothing of renewable power. The International Search Authority has recently issued International Search Report confirming its novelty, inventive step, and industrial applicability.
